Climate Controlled Storage:
You can help protect your personal belongings or business property from extreme hot and cold temperatures with our climate controlled units.

All U-Store facilities have password-protected gates to help control and monitor entry to the facility as well as a closed circuit camera system. 

Drive-Up Storage:
Many locations offer drive-up storage units to make loading and unloading your items easier. Some of these drive-up storage units are even located inside of a climate-controlled building.

Legend Statement  

At U-Store, we believe that doing right by our clients can and should also mean doing right by our world. Our company's core values of accountability, integrity, innovation, safety, teamwork, customer satisfaction, and always being involved with our community are not only meant to serve as guiding principles for our business and operations, but to also ensure we consistently deliver long-term value to all of our stakeholders. 

We aspire to reduce our environmental footprint, govern honestly, and increase the positive impact our business has on our clients, their communities, and our employees, by proactively incorporating sustainable business principles into our decision-making. That includes providing a high-quality product to our consumers, rewarding jobs with competitive benefits and compensations for our employees, and giving back to our communities.

     We believe that by giving back to our communities we will contribute to the betterment of the communities in which we do business and where our employees/clients live. We aim to make meaningful and lasting impacts through charitable giving, monthly money raffles, and community employee engagement. With that in mind, we U-Store are your local moving and storage resource.
